Prime Minister’s Youth Laptop Scheme (PMYLS) – Phase IV 
This is to inform the listed students that the distribution of laptops under the Prime 
Minister’s Youth Laptop Scheme (PMYLS) – Phase IV is scheduled as per the following 
details: 
 
Date: 16th – 18th February 2026 
 
Venue: Senate Hall, VC Secretariat, NED University of Engineering & Technology 
 
Time: As mentioned against each student’s name in the attached list 
All concerned students are advised to carefully check their allotted time and ensure 
their presence accordingly. 
Required Documents (Mandatory: Failure may result in non-issuance of the laptop) 
1. University ID Card (Original along with one photocopy) 
2. CNIC (Original along with one photocopy) 
3. One passport-size photograph 
4. Transcript / Academic record till Fall 2025 
 Only the concerned student is permitted to collect the laptop. Laptops will not be 
handed over to relatives or representatives. 
 All recipients are advised to use the stairs located at the backside of the Senate 
Hall. 
 Commute Advisory: Students who are scheduled to receive the awarded laptops 
are hereby advised to make appropriate and secure commuting arrangements on 
the day of distribution. A safe and reliable mode of transportation is desired to 
ensure the secure handling of the laptop and its accessories while returning 
home.
