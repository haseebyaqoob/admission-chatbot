VICE CHANCELLOR'S MESSAGE
==========================

"100 Years of Excellence in Engineering Education"

With heartfelt gratitude to Allah Almighty, I am truly honored and
privileged to serve as the Vice-Chancellor of this esteemed
institution—NED University of Engineering and Technology—which stands
as a distinguished symbol of resilience, innovation, and national
service. As we proudly celebrate a century of transformative impact,
the University reflects a legacy built upon academic rigor, societal
contribution, and a deep commitment to engineering excellence.


ACADEMIC PORTFOLIO
-------------------
  - 107 degree programmes (including 35 undergraduate offerings)
  - ~13,000 students
  - Graduates equipped with theoretical knowledge, creativity,
    analytical acumen, and practical expertise


FACULTY
--------
  - Exceptional, diverse, and dedicated
  - Many hold doctoral degrees from renowned international institutions


NATIONAL CENTERS OF EXCELLENCE
-------------------------------
  - National Incubation Center
  - National Centre for Cloud Computing and Data Analytics
  - National Center for Artificial Intelligence (NCAI)
  - National Center for Cybersecurity (NCCS)
  - National Center for Robotics and Automation (NCRA)


CAMPUSES
---------
  - Main Campus (University Road)
  - Latif Ebrahim Jamal (LEJ) Campus (Stadium Road)
  - City Campus (Maulana Din Muhammad Wafai Road)
  - Thar Institute of Engineering, Sciences and Technology (TIEST)
    in Tharparkar (regional outreach for underserved communities)


VISION
-------
  - Education beyond technical instruction
  - Socially responsible, ethically grounded, forward-thinking professionals
  - Critical thinking, leadership, communication, problem-solving
  - Interdisciplinary learning, entrepreneurial spirit,
    engagement with real-world challenges


ALUMNI
-------
  - Cornerstone of NED's enduring influence
  - Leadership positions in engineering, academia, business, government
  - Entrepreneurs driving innovation and employment
  - Spanning continents and industries


CLOSING
--------
As we celebrate 100 years of progress, we also embrace the challenges
and possibilities of the future. With renewed determination, NED
University is poised to lead national transformation—advancing research,
fostering sustainable innovation, and expanding its impact. Guided by
integrity, inclusivity, and excellence, we move forward to shape a
future where education becomes the catalyst for meaningful change and
national development.


Prof. Dr. Muhammad Tufail
Vice-Chancellor
