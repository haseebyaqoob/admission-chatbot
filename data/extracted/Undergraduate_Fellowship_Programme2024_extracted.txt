FOR CURRENT STUDENTS (2ND SEMESTER AND ONWARDS) 
• The students registered in various semesters shall be awarded these fellowships as per 
their CGPA standings in their respective semester examination results. 
• 1st, 2nd and 3rd position holders of each programme in every semester shall be eligible for 
Jinnah, Iqbal and Sir Syed fellowships respectively. All remaining students shall be awarded 
Jauhar Fellowship and financial assistance shall be available for students with less than 2.0 
CGPA. 
• In case there are shared positions, then the tie breaker will be based on the number of A+ 
grades, A grades, B grades and so on. Still, in case of continued tie, all students sharing the 
position shall become eligible for the respective fellowship. However, the subsequent 
fellowships shall override. 
• The CGPAs at the end of regular semesters (Fall and Spring semesters) shall be considered 
for awarding of fellowships in subsequent semesters.
