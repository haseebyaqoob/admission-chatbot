No.3 (2)/DG (Stats)/HEC/2017/03 Dated: 16-01-2017

Standardization of Mandatory Requirements for Inclusion in PhD Thesis: and

Notification.

Subject:

 

It has been observed with great concern that the standard for approval of PhD thesis
and notification for award of PhD degree varies from university to university.

To resolve the issue once for all, following standardized minimum requirements for
award of PhD degree have been approved by the competent authority to be fulfilled and
should be part of PhD thesis:

i) PhD thesis Author’s declaration.
ii) | Plagiarism undertaking by the PhD Scholar.
iii) Certificate of Approval of PhD thesis by Examination Committee.

Similarly draft Notification for award of PhD degree is enclosed for implementation.
These minimum requirements for award of PhD degree, are mandatory for the PhD degrees
awarded after 31% January, 2017. In case these minimum requirements are not met, name of
PhD Awardee will not be entered in the PhD Country Directory (PCD), which is mandatory for
attestation of PhD degree.

Enclosed specimen of:

i) PhD thesis Author’s declaration.

ii) Plagiarism undertaking by the PhD Scholar. r

iii) Certificate of Approval of PhD thesis by Examination Committee.
iv) Notification for award of PhD degree.
