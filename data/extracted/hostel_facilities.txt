NED UNIVERSITY - HOSTEL FACILITIES
====================================

The University provides residential accommodation for outstation students
through Muhammad Bin Qasim (MBQ) Hostel, located adjacent to the main
campus. This facility consists of two separate blocks for boys (MBQ-I and
MBQ-II) and a dedicated Girls Hostel.

Accommodation is primarily reserved for students who are non-residents of
Karachi and is allocated through a Hostel Allotment Committee appointed by
the Vice-Chancellor.


1. HOSTEL INFRASTRUCTURE
-------------------------

Hostel         Type of Room    No. of Rooms    Room Size              Capacity
------         -------------   -------------   -------------------    --------
MBQ-I          Cubicle         16              12'x8'6" = 102 sq ft   16 students
               Dormitory       34              18'6"x11' = 203 sq ft

MBQ-II         Cubicle         48              12'x8'6" = 102 sq ft   48 students
               Dormitory       72              18'6"x11' = 203 sq ft

Girls Hostel   Dormitory       20              12'x16' = 192 sq ft    41 students


2. FACILITIES & SERVICES
-------------------------

  - Common Room
  - Prayer Hall
  - Warden's Office
  - Canteen service (hygienic meals and refreshments)
  - Wi-Fi connectivity throughout the hostel premises
  - Washing and laundry services
  - Hostel Management Information System (HMIS) for computerised records


3. ALLOTMENT PROCESS
---------------------

Eligibility:
  - Students admitted from boards other than Karachi

Basis:
  - Transparent and merit-based
  - First come, first served

Timeline:
  - Applications invited in November from newly admitted first-year
    students (after completion of admission process)
  - Merit list maintained

Room Assignment:
  - First-year students initially allotted in hostel halls
  - Shifted to rooms as they become available (according to merit)
  - Dormitories: 3 students per room (due to shortage)
  - Cubicle rooms: reserved for final-year students


4. ADDITIONAL NOTES
--------------------

  - Boarding (mess) facilities are NOT provided by the University
    (canteen service available within hostel premises)
  - Residents get Wi-Fi for online academic resources
  - Washing and laundry facilities available
  - All records computerised via Hostel Management Information System (HMIS)
  - Hostel charges collected in advance on a semester basis
