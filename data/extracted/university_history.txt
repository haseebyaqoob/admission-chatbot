NED UNIVERSITY - HISTORY & BACKGROUND
======================================


1. ESTABLISHMENT
-----------------
The NED University of Engineering & Technology was established in
March 1977 under an act of the Provincial Assembly of Sindh, after
upgrading the former NED Government Engineering College, which was
set up in 1921. It is one of the oldest institutions in Pakistan for
teaching and producing Engineering graduates.


2. HISTORICAL TIMELINE
-----------------------

1921 (Aug 29):
  - College Principal C.S. Shahani pushed to start Engineering Degree
    classes to meet Civil Engineer demand for the Sukkur Barrage project
  - Application made to the University of Bombay through the
    Commissioner of Sindh
  - Bombay University rejected it (insufficient finance, needed
    separate buildings and labs)

Funding:
  - Donations from Puribai and Becharbai Trust
  - Vishandas Fatehchand Brothers
  - Rs. 150,000 from the family of Nadirshaw Edulji Dinshaw
    (a massive donation for that time)

1922:
  - 78 students provisionally admitted into first-year classes

1923 (May 23):
  - NED College provisionally granted affiliation by University of Bombay
    for first and second year Civil Engineering courses
  - Originally named "Prince of Wales Engineering College"
  - Later renamed in memory of Nadirshaw Edulji Dinshaw (NED)

1923 (Jul 1):
  - Mr. G.N. Gokhale joined as first full-time Principal
  - Prior: Rai Sahib Bhupatrai acted as Honorary Principal
  - Mr. S.B. Jannarkar: first Professor and Vice Principal

1927 (Feb):
  - Permanent affiliation granted

1922-1947:
  - Affiliated to the University of Bombay

1947:
  - Taken over by the Government of Sindh
  - Renamed: NED Government Engineering College
  - Affiliated to the University of Sindh

1951:
  - Affiliation transferred to the University of Karachi

1964:
  - Plan to shift from congested downtown area to a new site
    adjoining the University of Karachi
  - World Bank assistance: Rs. 118 million (two phases)

1975:
  - College shifted to new 40-hectare Main Campus

1977 (Mar 1):
  - Became NED University of Engineering & Technology


3. ORIGINAL BUILDINGS
----------------------
Four blocks and two sheds:
  - Main Block: Seth Fatehchand Dewandas Khilnani Hall
  - Power House, Electrical & Hydraulics Labs, Workshops:
    named after Bai Puribai and Bacharbai
  - Fourth block (1945): Classroom, Clerk's Office,
    Civil Drawing Hall
  - Shed 1: Carpentry and Smithy Shops
  - Shed 2: Electrical Lab and Engine Room (technician training)

Cost:
  - Buildings: just over Rs. 265,000
  - Equipment, books, furniture: just under Rs. 400,000


4. GROWTH
----------
  - 1923: 50 students enrolled
  - Present: ~11,986 students (UG and PG)
  - Bio-Medical Engineering at LEJ Campus
    (land & building worth Rs. 350 million donated by
     Late Latif Ebrahim Jamal)


5. TIEST - THAR INSTITUTE OF ENGINEERING, SCIENCES & TECHNOLOGY
-----------------------------------------------------------------
  - Established: October 2019
  - Location: Mithi, district Tharparkar, Sindh (~350 km East of Karachi)
  - Purpose: Quality education for deprived students in the area;
    produce workforce for coal excavation and power generation industries
  - Programmes launched:
    - BS Computer Science (2019)
    - Civil Engineering (2021)
    - Mechanical, Electrical, Chemical, Mining Engineering (upcoming)
