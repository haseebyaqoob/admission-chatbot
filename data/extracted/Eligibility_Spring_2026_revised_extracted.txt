E L I G I B I L I T Y 
1. The applicant must have passed their Qualifying Examinations Bachelor of Engineering Degree 
examination in the relevant Engineering Discipline from NED University or an equivalent recognized 
Institution with First Division (for Annual System) or at least with CGPA 2.40 / 4.0 (for Semester System).  
 
2. All Professional Bachelors Degree must be accredited by respective Accreditation bodies e.g. Pakistan 
Engineering Council (PEC) and Pakistan Council of Architects & Town Planners (PCATP)  
 
3. All other degrees must be recognized by Higher Education Commission (HEC).
