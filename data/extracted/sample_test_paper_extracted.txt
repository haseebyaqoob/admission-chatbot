Those privileged members of the community who have been through a secondary or public school 
education may be expected to know something about the elementary physics and chemistry of a hundred 
years ago, but they probably know hardly more than any bright boy can pick up from an interest in wireless 
or scientific hobbies out of school hours. As to the learning of scientific method, the whole thing is

astrology, not to say more dangerous ones such as racial theories or currency myths, shows that fifty years 
of education in the method of science in Britain or Germany has produced no visible effect whatever.
