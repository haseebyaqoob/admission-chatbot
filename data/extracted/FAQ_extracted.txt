Admission form

How can I get Admission Form? 
A. 
Application for the Undergraduate Admissions is available at Admission Web 
Portal www.neduet.edu.pk/admission. 
Q2. 
Who is eligible to apply for Admission? 
A. 
An applicant who is appearing in HSC-II or Equivalent Examination in 2026 and 
have passed his/her previous examination. 
An applicant who has passed HSC-II or Equivalent Examination in 2025, 2024 or 2023.

have secured at least 57% in HSC-1 or Equivalent Examinations (O-Levels for Cambridge 
Examination Board) are eligible to apply. However, they must maintain a minimum of 60% 
in their HSC/Equivalent examinations to confirm their admission. 
For all BS Programmes except BS (Computer Science) minimum 50% in HSC or 
Equivalent Examinations is required.

Q4. 
What is the merit criteria for merit for admission in undergraduate programmes? 
A. 
The overall merit in respect of candidates from admission shall be determined based on 
Merit Score (MS) to be calculated as: 
MS = 40% (HSC Part I/ O-level Marks/ Equivalent Examinations) * + 60% (NED Entry 
Test Marks) 
* For candidates already completed HSC/A-level/Equivalent examinations will be dealt as per policy. 
Q5. How should I take an additional Non-Credit (NC) course based on my admitted program 
and my intermediate/equivalent group? 
A.  The University will offer an additional NC course in First year, Fall semester, and 
students must qualify this course. The University will charge an additional fee for this 
NC course. 
Q6. I have been appearing in additional mathematics/improvement examinations, which 
marks/year I have to enter in the admission form? 
A.  You have to enter the HSC/Equivalent examination marks/year which you have 
completed or for which you have the complete result in hand by the last date of online 
submission of admission application. 
Q7. What to do, if I am unable to register on Admission Web Portal? or email address is 
already registered? Or User Name and Password not received via email? 
A.  You have to re-check that either you have already registered with the same email 
address or CNIC number. If you did not receive the login information, please check Junk 
or Spam folder in your email. In both cases, please contact on Helpdesk to resolve the 
issue.

Q8.  What to do, if I am unable to login to the Admission Portal with the provided User ID 
and Password? 
A.  Make sure to enter the correct User ID and Password, since the password is case 
sensitive. Also make sure the difference between “0” (zero) and “O” (O for orange), “L” 
(L for Leaf) or “I” (I for Ink) which usually cause password error. 
Q9.  What if, I am unable to upload my photograph on the Admission Portal? 
A. 
Please make sure that your photograph is according to the instructions provided in 
“Guideline for the Application Photograph”. 
Q10. How can I pay Application Fee? 
A. 
Following procedure is to be followed for the payment of the application fee: 
i) 
After completing Online Application Form, required application fee (including self- 
finance/sponsor fee, if applicable) shall be indicated on the Transaction Slip, which 
contains an important number called Transaction ID. 
ii) 
Take a Print-out of Transaction Slip and pay the indicated fee at any branch of the banks 
specified on Transaction Slip. 
iii) 
It is very important to note that the payment at the bank should be against the 
Transaction ID mentioned on the Transaction Slip. 
Q11. How would I get my Pre-Admission Entry Test ADMIT CARD? 
A. 
After confirmation of receipt of fee at the bank, ADMIT CARD will be available in the 
Online Admission Portal, provided all other requirements (mainly photograph) found 
suitable. Please note that Admit Card will be not issued if either the fee payment is 
not confirmed or uploaded photograph is invalid. 
Q12. What to do after passing Pre-Admission Entry Test? 
A. 
You are required to follow the admission schedule and instructions given on it. For 
updates kindly refer to NED University website: https://www.neduet.edu.pk/admission. 
Q13. Where Admission Form can be submitted? 
A. 
You need to print out the Admission Form along with Annexures (I & II), duly signed 
from Oath Commissioner and submit with other required documents to Admission 
Office of the University at the time of interview. Please refer the Document Checklist 
for further details. 
Q14. How can I apply for any additional category? 
A. 
You can apply against any additional category for which you are eligible during initial 
submission of online Admission Form, latest by the due date. 
Note: Eligibility for admission under each category/discipline is as described in Chapter-4 & 
Chapter-5 of the Undergraduate Prospectus (Soft copy is available at NED University 
website).

Q15. Do I have to pay any fee for Additional category? 
A. 
No, there is NO extra fee for additional category(ies) in which you are eligible to apply 
except Self-Finance category(ies). 
Q16. What are the requirements of admission in the category of differently able candidate? 
A. 
For admission in category for those having disability and passed HSC (Pre- 
Engineering/Pre-Medical/Computer Science/Arts/Arts-Math/Commerce) part I and II (as 
the case may be), from any Board of Intermediate of Education situated in province of 
Sindh and having certificate of disability from Department of Empowerment of Persons 
with Disabilities (DEPD), Government of Sindh. Further the candidate is required to 
appear before the Medical Board at the University. 
Q17. What are the admission requirements for the category of religious minorities? 
A. 
They must be resident of Pakistan, belong to religious minorities and have passed HSC 
(Pre-Engineering/ Pre-Medical / Computer Science / Commerce / Arts /Arts-Math) Part I 
and II from any Board of Intermediate Education or Equivalent foreign examination as 
recognized by the University can apply for the religious minorities category. 
Q18. Are DAE holders eligible to apply for the admissions? 
A. 
For admission in the category reserved for Diploma of Associate Engineer (DAE), the 
applicant must pass the qualifying examination from institution recognized by Sindh 
Board of Technical Education (SBTE), with minimum 60%, during last three years. 
Applicants who are awaiting the result are not eligible to apply. 
 
Entry Test 
 
Q1.  What are the passing marks in Entry Tests? 
A. 
50% marks in entry test are required to qualify for admission. 
Q2.  When would the Entry test be held? 
A. 
Entry Test schedule will be available at www.neduet.edu.pk/admission. 
Q3. When would Entry Test result be announced? 
A. 
Pre-Admission Entry test result will be announced after the last session of the computer- 
based test and available on NEDUET website. 
Q4. How many times a candidate may appear in the entry test? 
A. 
Pre-Admission Entry test will be held twice. A candidate can appear in both tests. 
Separate fee for each test is required to pay. 
Q5. Which Pre-Admission Entry test score will be considered for the admission? 
A. 
Between the two results, whichever is higher will be considered for the admission. 
Q6.  How much fee will be charged for the second attempt? 
A. 
The test fee for second attempt of entry test will be notified on NED website.

Q7.  What will be dates for the Pre-Admission Entry Phase-I and Phase-II? 
A. 
The test dates for both phases will be notified on NED website.

Q4. What if I will get admission in Regular scheme, would the self-finance amount be 
refunded? 
A.  Yes, when you opt for admission in Regular Seat, total self-finance fee will be refunded. 
For refund you have to submit self-finance refund form (available at website) with all 
relevant documents as required. 
Q5.  Does Self-finance fee include admission fee? 
A.   No, it does not. Self-finance fee is to be paid once only at the time of admission form 
submission. You must have to pay admission fee at the time of admission in addition to 
the Self-Finance fee.

A.

A.

interview/admission. The admission fee may in the form of Cash or Pay order/Demand 
draft in favor of Director Finance, NEDUET.

Miscellaneous 
 
Q1. 
Where Bachelor of Architecture and BS Development Studies classes are held? 
A. 
Classes of Bachelor of Architecture and BS Development Studies are held at City 
Campus which is adjacent to Govt. DJ Science College. 
Classes for Bio-Medical Engineering are held at LEJ Campus situated opposite to Liaquat 
National Hospital. 
Q2. 
Is there any Shuttle Bus Service available for City Campus and LEJ Campus? 
A. 
No, there is no specific bus service for these campuses. However, some of the shuttle 
buses pass in front of these campuses. Details will be provided on the day of orientation. 
Q3. 
What is the duration of degree programmes offered by the University? 
A. 
All programmes are of Four years duration except Bachelors in Architecture which is of 
Five years duration.
