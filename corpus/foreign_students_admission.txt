ADMISSION OF FOREIGN STUDENTS
=============================

1. Foreign Students
-------------------
The NED University does not entertain any admission request from Foreigners
directly. Foreigners are admitted against reserved seats if their names are
recommended by the Higher Education Commission of Pakistan (HEC) OR Economic
Affairs Division (Ministry of Education, Government of Pakistan) (EAD).


2. Eligibility Criteria
-----------------------

a. Pre-requisite Qualification
   - Higher Secondary Certificate (HSC) Pre-Engineering
     (Physics, Chemistry and Mathematics)
   - OR equivalent qualification with Physics, Chemistry and Mathematics
   - NED University does NOT accept any qualification without subjects
     Physics, Chemistry and Mathematics
   - Must have passed HSC Pre-Engineering with at least 60% aggregate marks

b. Pre-Admission Entry Test
   - Must qualify the University's Pre-Admission Entry Test based on HSC
     level subjects: English, Physics, Chemistry and Mathematics
   - Foreigners and Overseas Pakistani candidates may take SAT examination
     instead of the entry test:
       * SAT I: minimum 800 score
       * SAT II: minimum 1500 score (in Physics, Chemistry and Mathematics)

c. English Language Skill
   - Tested through the Pre-Admission Entry Test
   - Medium of instruction in all subjects is English

------------------

directly, Foreigners are admitted against reserved seats only if their names
are recommended by:

   - OR Economic Affairs Division (EAD),
     Ministry of Education, Government of Pakistan
