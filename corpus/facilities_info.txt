NED UNIVERSITY - FACILITIES
============================

1. ENGR. ABUL KALAM LIBRARY
----------------------------
Established in 1921, one of Pakistan's leading academic libraries focusing on
engineering sciences and technology. Serves as the regional reference library
of engineering and technology.

Library Collection:
  - General circulation books (textbooks)
  - Reference books (encyclopedias, handbooks, dictionaries, manuals)
  - Periodicals (current subscriptions + previous journal volumes)
  - Government documents & archive material
    (Acts, Ordinances, Service Rules, Statistical reports, Census reports)
  - Survey reports, Planning reports, Budgets
  - Audio Visual content (CDROMs, DVDs)
  - Digital content (eBooks, eJournals)

Security:
  - State-of-the-art RFID-based security system

Department Libraries:
  - Located at City Campus, LEJ Campus, and Main Campus
  - NED Academy library for CCEE and CMPP students

Book Bank Service (Undergraduates):
  - Textbooks provided at 10% of cost for the entire semester

Lending Library / Duty Society (Undergraduates):
  - Textbooks provided free of cost for the entire semester

Technology:
  - Computerised Library Management System, Website, OPAC, Portal
  - 65 latest-configuration computers
  - Digital services: e-Books, e-Journals (HEC National Digital Library),
    laser printing, scanning, CD/DVD writing, copying

Mission: User-oriented learning environment, preserve universal knowledge.


2. INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY (IT) SERVICES
----------------------------------------
Established: 1998-99

NEDUET Campus Management System (CMS):
  - Paperless environment for admin & academic departments
  - Systems: Undergraduate/PG Student Management, HR Management,
    Attendance, Security Operations, Academic Performance Monitoring
  - Journal and research databanks
  - Single sign-on 24/7 portal (NEDUET Campus Portal)
  - Online semester registration for UG & PG students

NEDUET Website:
  - Dedicated Web Development Team
  - Supports departmental websites

Metro LAN:
  - First in educational public sector
  - 37 km Optical Fibre Metro Area Network (MAN)
  - Connects Main, LEJ & City Campuses

NEDUET ISP:
  - 24x7 high-speed Internet access
  - Dial-up service available
  - Network Operation Centre (NOC) with enterprise-grade equipment

NEDUET WiFi:
  - Campus-wide coverage via iAccess captive portal
  - Authentication, accounting services

Video Conferencing:
  - Centrally-located facility for virtual classes, conferences, seminars
  - Available 24/7, local and international connectivity

Computational Services (HPC):
  - First public sector university in Pakistan to build own clusters
  - Two HPC clusters:
    - 50 nodes (32-bit architecture)
    - 32 virtualised nodes (64-bit architecture)
  - Accessible from all campuses + remotely via internet

Software Repository:
  - Generic and application-oriented licensed software
  - Standalone: HPC Centre only | Network-based: entire university

Training:
  - Trainings, seminars, workshops for staff, faculty, and students

IT Help Desk:
  - Software & Desktop Support
  - Network Support
  - Hardware Maintenance
  - NEDUET-CMS (Portal)
  - High Performance Computing (HPC)
  - Services extended to all three campuses


3. INSTRUMENTATION CENTRE
--------------------------
Established under HEC grant. Provides testing & calibration services.

Capabilities:
  - Calibration of multimeters, energy meters,
    pressure/temperature gauges, water quality, flow
  - Training for UG and PG students
  - Assists final year projects and R&D activities

Laboratories:
  - Electronics and Signals Lab
  - Pressure and Temperature Lab
  - Equipment Testing Lab
  - Water Quality and Flow Testing Lab
  - Virtual Instrumentation Lab (National Instruments LabVIEW)

Accreditation:
  - Pursuing ISO 17025 accreditation
  - Goal: Regional leader in test & calibration
  - Will provide services to industries/organisations in Karachi area


4. NED ACADEMY
---------------
Established: 1 July 2008 (CCEE founded 1998)

Objectives:
  - Update engineers with recent developments
  - Enhance employability of working engineers
  - Help lower-tier professionals meet registration levels
  - Structured programmes for engineers unable to pursue formal Masters
  - Postgraduate diplomas in specific technologies (sugar, plastics, etc.)

Two Sections:
  - Centre for Continuing Engineering Education (CCEE)
    - University-Industry partnerships, continuing education courses
  - Centre for Multidisciplinary Postgraduate Programmes (CMPP)
    - Long-duration certificate courses, PG Certificate Courses


5. FERROCEMENT INTERNATIONAL NETWORK (FIN-PAKISTAN)
----------------------------------------------------
  - National Node for ferrocement, established 1990 in Civil Engineering Dept.
  - Under International Node at IFIC-AIT-BANGKOK
  - Disburses research material and disseminates knowledge in ferrocement


6. COWASJEE EARTHQUAKE STUDY CENTRE (CESNED)
----------------------------------------------
  - Established 2001 after Bhuj earthquake
  - Under Department of Earthquake Engineering
  - Houses national & global earthquake data
  - Disseminates earthquake knowledge
  - Responds to emergency needs
  - Guiding principles for pre/post disaster mitigation
  - Recognised as one of two earthquake study centres in Pakistan
    (after 2005 Kashmir earthquake)


7. SHAKE TABLE TESTING FACILITY
---------------------------------
  - State-of-the-art facility in Earthquake Engineering Department
  - Simulates real-time earthquake ground motion
  - Records dynamic response of structures


8. ADVANCED MATERIAL TESTING FACILITY
--------------------------------------
  - State-of-the-art facility in Earthquake Engineering Department
  - Full-scale static and pseudo-dynamic testing
  - Tests structural components of buildings and bridges


9. PCB FABRICATION LABORATORY
-------------------------------
  - Located in Electronic Engineering Department
  - Trains UG students in PCB fabrication technology
  - Supported by PCB layout design and auto-routing software
  - Fabricates PCBs for local requirements


10. PRODUCT DEVELOPMENT CENTRE (PDC)
-------------------------------------
  - Fully equipped with sophisticated equipment and software
  - Used for re-engineering


11. DIRECTORATE OF INDUSTRIAL LIAISON (DIL)
--------------------------------------------
Established: 1997

Activities:

  - Arranges technical/practical help from industries

  - Study visits for students and faculty


12. MEDICAL DEPARTMENT
-----------------------
Staff:
  - 6 doctors total
    - Main Campus: 2 morning, 1 evening
    - LEJ Campus: 1
    - City Campus: 1 lady doctor
  - Headed by Principal Medical Officer
  - Paramedical staff (male & female nurses)

Facilities (free for students at Main Campus):
  - Emergency and Outpatient services
  - Pathological, Radiological, ECG labs
  - Airway, breathing, circulatory intervention
  - X-Ray, Pathology Lab, Ambulance (Main Campus only)
  - Outpatient at City and LEJ Campus
